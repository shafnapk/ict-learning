Kozhikode is situated in southern India between the Western Ghats and the Arabian Sea, Kozhikode has been attracting travelers from antiquity. The city is also known by its Anglicized name, Calicut.

Tourist Hotspots

kallayi

Kallai is a small town on the banks of Kallai River which links with the Chaliyar river on the south by a man-made canal.

Kozhikode Beach

Kozhikode Beach is a beach on the western side of Kozhikode.There is one Lions Park for the children and an aquarium.

Pazhassiraja Archaeological Museum

It is a museum and art gallery in Kozhikode, Kerala. The museum has a rich collection of historical artifacts from 1000 BC to 200 AD..
